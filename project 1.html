My Favourite Book

Harry Potter and The Goblet of Fire , First Edition, By J K Rowling in 2000

Article by Aafreen

Here's a quote from the Author J K Rowling:

I don't believe in the kind of magic in my books. But I do believe something very magical can happen when you read a good book

Here are some of the reasons I just loved the book

  • The Quidditch World Cup Gives Us a Glimpse into the Wizarding World
  • Harry Gets to Fight a Dragon and That Is Awesome
  • The Yule Ball Is Basically the Prom We All Wish We Had
  • Hermione Finally Gets the Attention She Deserves(which is frankly, the most important thing for me)
  • Other Wizard Schools Are Introduced (And So Is a World of Magical Possibility)
  • It Marks the Beginning of the Darkness that Overtakes the Series
  • It has a great soundtrack
